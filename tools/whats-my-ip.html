FAQ

Is this my private (local) IP address?

No. This page shows your public IP (internet-facing). Your private IP usually looks like 192.168.x.x, 10.x.x.x, or 172.16–172.31.x.x.

Will this page store my IP address?

No. This tool runs in your browser and fetches your IP from a simple endpoint. Check the Privacy page for details.
