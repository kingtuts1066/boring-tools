Band / valuation lookups (official)

Council tax systems differ by nation. These links help you find your band/valuation.

  • England & Wales: Valuation Office Agency (VOA) band search: gov.uk/council-tax-bands
  • Scotland: Scottish Assessors band search: saa.gov.uk
  • Northern Ireland: Domestic rates (not bands): nidirect.gov.uk/articles/domestic-rates
What is this page (and what isn’t it)?

This is a simple calculator to help you budget and sanity-check instalments. It does not calculate your band, fetch your council’s charges, or replace your bill.
