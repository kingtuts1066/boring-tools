FAQ

Why does this differ from my local DNS results?

Resolvers can differ (caching, geo-routing, filtering). This tool queries the chosen public resolver via HTTPS from your browser.

Can this check DNS propagation?

Partially. You can switch resolvers, but true propagation checks require querying many resolvers worldwide.
